About

Serenity Foot & Body has worked the same corner of Sheung Wan for eight years. The team blends Cantonese tui na technique with modern sports-recovery pressure work, and every treatment starts with a herbal foot soak.

Two minutes from Sheung Wan MRT exit A2, above the dried-seafood street. Look for the jade signboard.
